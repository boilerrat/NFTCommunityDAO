# Vision and Values

## Our Vision

{% hint style="info" %}
**Good to know:** a good vision statement shows the long-term goals of the company without getting too deep into strategy, implementation, or product specifics.
{% endhint %}

Our vision is to support NFT artists amd communities through investments and support. We aim to provide services enabling NFT communties to form decentralized autonomous organizations roung the NFT project that the community is supporting.

As NFT collectors and enthusiasts ourselves we also aim to use this DAO as a way to collect NFTs that we enjoy.
